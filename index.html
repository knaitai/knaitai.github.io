A glimpse of knowledge-native AI

From predicting motion
to understanding behavior.

A vessel’s recent track suggests several possible destinations. But movement alone is not the whole story. KnaiTai brings relevant knowledge into the reasoning itself—shipping corridors, vessel requirements, port capabilities, geography and prior behavior—and makes visible how each changes the conclusion.

In this synthetic maritime example, an observation-only model initially favors Tanjung Pelepas. As relevant knowledge becomes active, the system evaluates the shipping lane, the vessel’s draft and berth requirements, candidate-port capabilities, protected waters and the vessel’s prior calls. The result is not merely a prediction—it is an inspectable account of what the system knew, what it used and why it mattered.
